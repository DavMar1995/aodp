This figure presents three stories about sources of foreign visitors to Japan during 2003 to 2019 in terms of number of people. The first story shows the total number of visitors to Japan by continent-of-origin during 2003 to 2019. The second story shows yearly number of visitors to Japan by continent-of-origin during 2003 to 2019. The last story uses geographic map with different colors to show yearly number of visitors to Japan by continent-of-origin during 2003 to 2019. 

These stories are derivatives of the dataset titled 国籍/月別 訪日外客数 provided by data.go.jp, licensed under CC-BY.
