Number of Tourist of Korea

This board present the number of tourist of South Korea from 2003 to June, 2020. In this board, the number of tourist have a substantially growth since 2003, but the number still slightly reduce in recent years.

Because of breakout of MERS in June, 2015, the number of tourists was lowered and the tourism industry was impacted. As for the reason why the number also decreased in 2017, citizens of mainland China were forbidden from traveling to South Korea due to the official restriction. The restriction continued until December the same year.

Whereas, the biggest downturn in Korean tourism industry is not the two cases mentioned above. 2020 is a nightmare for all human beings due to COVID-19. Its influence spreads to plenty of countries while South Korea is on the list. The number of tourists has hit the bottom.
